Report Issues or Unprofessional Behavior

We maintain high standards and take all reports seriously

Report a Professional

If a professional has acted unprofessionally, failed to deliver services, or violated our terms

  • • Unprofessional behavior or communication
  • • Failure to deliver promised services
  • • Violation of agreement terms
  • • Inappropriate conduct

Report a Client

If a client has been abusive, refused payment, or violated our terms

  • • Non-payment or payment disputes
  • • Abusive or disrespectful behavior
  • • Unreasonable demands or expectations
  • • Violation of platform guidelines

How to Report

To report any issues or unprofessional behavior, contact our support team using any of the methods above. Please provide as much detail as possible including:

  • • Names of involved parties
  • • Description of the incident
  • • Date and time of occurrence
  • • Any supporting evidence (screenshots, messages, etc.)

All reports are kept confidential and will be investigated promptly. We are committed to maintaining a safe and professional environment for all users.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I create a profile?

Sign up for an account, verify your email, then create either a Professional or Client profile from your dashboard.

How do payments work?

We use secure M-Pesa integration for payments. All transactions are processed safely through our platform.

How do I contact other users?

Use our secure messaging system to communicate with other professionals and clients through your dashboard.

What if I have a dispute?

Contact our support team immediately. We have processes in place to resolve disputes fairly and quickly.

How do I verify my profile?

Upload required documents and wait for admin verification. Verified profiles get better visibility and trust.

Can I have multiple profiles?

Yes, you can create both Professional and Client profiles under the same account as needed.
